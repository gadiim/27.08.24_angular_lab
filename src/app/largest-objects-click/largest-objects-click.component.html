The largest car is...

The world's longest car is "The American Dream," which is 100 feet and 1.5 inches long. It's a super limo with 26 wheels, a helipad, a putting green, and a swimming pool. The car was built by Jay Ohrberg in 1986 and started out as six Cadillac Eldorados.

The largest plane is...

The Antonov An-225 Mriya, a Ukrainian cargo aircraft that entered service in 1988. In terms of length, wingspan and maximum take-off weight, it is the largest in the world, with a length of 84 metres, a wingspan of 88 metres and a maximum take-off weight of 640 tonnes.
